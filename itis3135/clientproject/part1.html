Design Proposal

Smoke Signals BBQ

The client is seeking a comprehensive website that showcases various cuts of beef and pork, along with cooking techniques for each. Additionally, the website will highlight different grilling accessories and methods, such as propane or charcoal grilling. Popular side dishes to accompany BBQ will also be featured, alongside noteworthy names in the BBQ industry. The website will be informative in nature and will feature diagrams, images, and videos showcasing different aspects of the BBQ experience. A color theme that captures the essence of BBQ will be integrated throughout the site to enhance user engagement.
