Hiking

Why hike?

Hiking is a great way to get outdoors, enjoy nature, and get some exercise. Not only does it provide a physical workout, but it can also be a great way to reduce stress and clear your mind. Hiking allows you to explore new places and see things that you might not see otherwise. It can be a solo activity or a social one, allowing you to spend time with friends or meet new people. Additionally, hiking can be an inexpensive way to spend time, as there are often many trails and parks that are free or have minimal entrance fees. So whether you're looking for a new way to exercise, a way to connect with nature, or just a way to spend some time outside, hiking can be a great option.

What should I bring?

When going on a hike, it is important to bring the necessary items to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. Some essential items to bring while hiking include appropriate footwear, a map or GPS device, plenty of water, snacks or a meal, sunscreen, insect repellent, a first aid kit, and appropriate clothing for the weather conditions. Bringing a backpack to carry these items is also recommended. It is important to plan ahead and pack appropriately for the length and difficulty of the hike, and to research the trail beforehand to determine any additional items that may be necessary. By bringing these essential items, hikers can enjoy their experience while staying safe and prepared for any potential challenges.

It's worth noting that the items you bring on a hike may vary depending on the length and difficulty of the trail you plan to undertake, as well as any potential hazards in the area. It's important to research the trail beforehand and consider any potential risks, such as extreme weather or wildlife encounters, and pack accordingly. In addition to the essential items mentioned previously, it's recommended to bring items such as a flashlight or headlamp, a whistle, a multi-tool or knife, and a charged mobile phone or emergency beacon. By being prepared and packing for any situation, hikers can enjoy a safe and fulfilling outdoor experience.
How I went about it

During my time in Asheville, I had the incredible opportunity to explore various hiking areas and soak up their breathtaking views. Careful planning was always key as we considered the weather conditions, hike difficulty, and necessary gear, all while keeping an eye out for the possibility of encountering black bears. To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience, we always packed ample water, snacks, portable chargers, tools, jackets, and bear spray. While our hikes typically lasted only a few hours, we never underestimated their potential difficulties. In fact, one hike that was considered hard resulted in challenging climbs and a delayed return after nightfall. Nevertheless, hiking remains one of my all-time favorite activities, evoking a sense of liberation and accomplishment upon reaching the summit.
